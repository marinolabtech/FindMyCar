**Never forget where you parked again.**

Find My Car is a premium Android app that saves your vehicle's parking spot automatically and guides you back with precision — wherever you left it.

---

## ✨ How it works

Park your car. Get out. That's it.

Find My Car detects when you disconnect from your car's Bluetooth or unplug the charging cable, and instantly saves your exact GPS location. When you need to find your car, just open the app — a real-time 3D compass points you straight to it.

No need to remember to open the app. No need to press anything. It just works.

## 🔑 Features

**Autoparking** *(in-app purchase)*
Saves your parking spot automatically when your car's Bluetooth disconnects or the power cable is unplugged. A smart 10-second delay with speed check prevents false positives — no accidental saves while driving.

**High-Precision GPS**
An intelligent positioning algorithm searches for the best GPS fix for up to 10 seconds, ensuring accuracy under 10 meters. When charging, GPS stays warm with updates every 5 seconds for an instant, pinpoint save.

**3D Compass Finder**
A premium compass with a physical bezel and real-time pointer guiding you on foot directly to your vehicle, with live distance display.

**Interactive Notifications**
A persistent status notification shows real-time GPS accuracy, Bluetooth connection status, and quick-action buttons to open the compass or maps — without even unlocking your phone.

**Works with any vehicle**
No car Bluetooth? No problem. Cable-only mode works with any vehicle, with or without in-car Bluetooth.

**Xiaomi / MIUI optimization**
Special handling for aggressive battery saving policies on Xiaomi devices, so the service always runs reliably in the background.
